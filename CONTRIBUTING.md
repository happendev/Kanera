## Conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and patient with other contributors and users. Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, or repeated bad-faith participation are not welcome in Kanera project spaces. Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, discussions, or other project channels to keep them useful and safe.

## Support

Community support is best-effort through GitHub issues or discussions when those channels are enabled. Please use security reporting for vulnerabilities instead of public issues. Commercial support, hosted-service support, private roadmap commitments, and custom licensing are handled separately by Happen Software Limited.
