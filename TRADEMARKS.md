If you distribute or publish a modified version of Kanera, you must:

- use a different product name;

- make it clear that your version is not the official Kanera project;
- preserve copyright, license, and required notices.

You may describe the project as "based on Kanera" or "a fork of Kanera" if that statement is accurate and presented in a non-misleading way.

- rebrand a fork or modified version as Kanera;

- register domains, social accounts, app listings, company names, or trademarks that are confusingly similar to Kanera.
